About Treatments and Decisions
In SAS Intelligent Decisioning , you use treatments to define offers that you might 
want to present to subjects that contact your company as a result of an inbound 
marketing campaign.
In an inbound marketing campaign, a subject initiates contact with a company 
through a specific channel such as email, a website, or a phone call. A subject can 
be an individual customer, a company account, a household, or other entity. 
Depending on the channel, an application or website captures the subject’s behavior 
or a human representative enters the subject’s information into a form or a customer 
service application.
